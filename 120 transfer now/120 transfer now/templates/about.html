Our Mission

Reuniting lost pets with their families through the power of artificial intelligence

Pet ID Malaysia was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to leverage cutting-edge AI technology to help pet owners find their missing companions. Every year, thousands of pets go missing in Malaysia, and we believe that technology can make a real difference in bringing them home safely.

Our Technology

Powered by state-of-the-art deep learning algorithms

Our AI system uses advanced computer vision and deep learning techniques to analyze pet photos. The technology is built on EfficientNetV2 architecture, specifically trained for pet facial recognition with over 95% accuracy rate.

State-of-the-art convolutional neural network optimized for accuracy and efficiency in pet recognition tasks.

Advanced algorithms that identify and extract unique facial features, patterns, and markings from pet photos.

Cosine similarity algorithms that compare extracted features against thousands of pets in our database for accurate matching.
