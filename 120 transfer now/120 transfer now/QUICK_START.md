# Quick Start Guide - Found Cats Feature

## For Users Who Found a Missing Cat

### Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Go to the Website**
   - Open Pet ID Malaysia in your browser

2. **Click "Found a Cat?"**
   - Look in the navigation menu at the top
   - Or click the green "Report Found Cat" button on the homepage

3. **Upload the Cat's Photo**
   - Drag and drop the image
   - Or click "Choose File" to browse
   - Supported formats: JPG, JPEG, PNG
   - Maximum size: 16MB

4. **Fill in the Details**
   - **Location** (Required): Where you found the cat
     - Example: "Near Central Park, Kuala Lumpur"
   - **Date Found**: When you found the cat (auto-filled with today)
   - **Description** (Optional): Any details about the cat
     - Example: "Orange tabby with white paws, very friendly"
   - **Contact Info** (Optional): How owners can reach you
     - Example: Your phone number or email

5. **Submit**
   - Click "Submit Found Cat"
   - Wait for processing (usually 5-10 seconds)

6. **Success!**
   - You'll see a confirmation page
   - The cat is now in the database
   - Pet owners can find it when they search

## For Pet Owners Looking for Lost Cats

### Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Go to the Homepage**
   - Open Pet ID Malaysia

2. **Upload Your Pet's Photo**
   - Drag and drop the image in the upload area
   - Or click "Choose File"
   - Use a clear photo of your cat's face

3. **Click "Find Matches"**
   - The AI will search the entire database
   - This includes all found cats reported by the community

4. **View Results**
   - You'll see top 3 matches
   - Each match shows:
     - Photo of the found cat
     - Similarity score (percentage)
     - Location where found
     - Date found
     - Contact information (if provided)

5. **Contact the Finder**
   - If you see your cat, use the contact information
   - Arrange to meet and verify it's your pet

## Browse Found Cats

### View All Found Cats

1. **Click "View Found Cats"** in the navigation menu

2. **Browse the Gallery**
   - See all cats that have been found
   - Cards show:
     - Cat photo
     - Location
     - Date found
     - Description

3. **Contact Finders**
   - Click "Contact Finder" button on any card
   - See contact information in the popup

## Common Questions

### Q: Is there a fee?
**A:** No, the service is completely free.

### Q: Do I need an account?
**A:** No, you can upload anonymously.

### Q: How long does it take?
**A:** Processing takes 5-10 seconds. Results are instant.

### Q: What if I don't want to share my contact info?
**A:** Contact information is optional. You can upload without it.

### Q: Can I edit my upload later?
**A:** Currently not supported. You can upload a new entry.

### Q: How accurate is the AI?
**A:** Our AI has 95% accuracy rate for cat facial recognition.

### Q: What happens to the photos?
**A:** Photos are stored securely and only used for matching purposes.

### Q: Can I delete my upload?
**A:** Currently not available via the website. Contact support.

### Q: What if the cat was reunited?
**A:** Great! The entry will remain in the database for records.
